Game One : THE MIMIC

The mimic is a game which goes with the horror genre, It envolves yokai stories as you go as the protagonist which eventually either encounters the main antagonist either because of your ancestors or a cult which eventually took your brother and made him into one of them. There is currently only 2 Books which are both meant to have 4 chapters each, book 2 chapter 4 is coming in development and is expected to arrive by this year.

Game Two : RIVALS

Rivals is a FPS game which consists of from 1v1s to 4v4s, there is a variety of weapons which you can use in order to eliminate the other team. The currency of this game consists of keys in which you can either use to design your weapon or to buy new weapons. the more you play the game the more levels you earn and if you continue winning you earn a streak, which you can lose if you lose if your opponent eliminates you 5 times, earning themselves 5 points.

Game Three : Flee The Facility

Flee the facility consists of a very simple cat and mouse chase styled game, there is a beast and there is 2-4 players running away from them, the beast tries to catch the players while the players hack computers in order to then get the exit open. Once the beast catches someone they put them in a frozen ccell which the players get slowly frozen to death yet can be saved bby other players.
