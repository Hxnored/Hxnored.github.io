BADMINTON

WHAT BADMINTON IS.

Badminton is a sport which is a racquet sport, meaning that it is similar in terms like tennis. The ball is a shuttlecock, a plastic piece in which acts as the thing you throw back at the opponent. there are two types of badminton, 2v2s and 1v1s which are either duo against duo or a person vs person. The way on playing this is really simple, just hit the shuttlecock without it hitting the net nor the ground, greater teams can be allowed but for competitive is mainly 1v1s or 2v2s as explained before.

.

HISTORY OF BADMINTON.

Sports in which the shuttlecock has been used at the ball have been played like that for centuries all over Eurasia. New modern era of badminton was created in the mid 19th century of british india as a different variant of the game battledore and shuttlecock, battledore being another name for racquet. The reason on why it is named "badminton" is due to Duke of Beaufort's Badminton House in Gloucestershire yet there is no other reason from that and there is unclear origins of this.

TOURNAMENTS IN BADMINTON

There has been several tournaments throughout badminton yet there has been some in which have been considered as important events. such as for example, the Thomas cup has been classified as important tournament which the first ever competition was hosted in 1948. due to this it has now been made every 2-3 years on which one on ones or two versus two are hosted. Another big tournament which was hosted is the badminton world cup all the way in 1899 which was founded by the EBA (England Badminton Association.) This is the oldest badminton tournament in HISTORY which was mainly hosten in the United Kingdom but is now global.
